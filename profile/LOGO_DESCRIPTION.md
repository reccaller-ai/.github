# RecCaller.AI Logo

## Design Elements

The RecCaller.AI logo combines three key concepts:

1. **Brain/Neural Network** - Represents AI intelligence and context understanding
   - Simplified brain shape in blue gradient
   - Neural network nodes connected with pathways
   - Symbolizes the ML-powered context management system

2. **Circular Arrow** - Represents "recall" and context retrieval
   - Circular motion suggests persistence and continuity
   - Arrow indicates direction and action (calling/retrieving context)
   - Located at the bottom of the brain, showing context flow

3. **Color Scheme** - Professional blue palette
   - Primary: `#3b82f6` (Bright blue) - Trust and technology
   - Accent: `#0ea5e9` (Cyan) - Innovation and AI
   - Background: `#1e40af` (Deep blue) - Professional and stable

## Logo Variants

- **logo.svg** - Standard version for light backgrounds
- **logo-dark.svg** - Enhanced version for dark backgrounds (brighter colors)

## Usage

The logo is designed to be:
- Scalable (SVG format)
- Professional and modern
- Memorable and distinctive
- Representative of AI and context management
